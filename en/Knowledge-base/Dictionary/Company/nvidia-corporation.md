# Overview

NVIDIA Corporation is a technology company that designs and manufactures graphics processing units (GPUs) for the gaming and professional markets, as well as system on a chip (SoC) units for the mobile computing and automotive markets. Founded in 1993 by Jensen Huang, NVIDIA is headquartered in Santa Clara, California, and has over 11,000 employees worldwide. NVIDIA's products are used in gaming, professional visualization, data centers, and automotive applications.
