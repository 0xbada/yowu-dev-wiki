# Description

Peter Thiel is an American entrepreneur, investor, and author. He is best known as the co-founder of PayPal, the online payment system, and as an early investor in Facebook. Thiel is also the founder and chairman of Palantir Technologies, a data analytics company, and a co-founder of Founders Fund, a venture capital firm.

Thiel is known for his contrarian views on various topics, including education, technology, and politics. He has been a vocal critic of higher education, arguing that it is overpriced and does not provide students with the skills they need to succeed in the modern economy. Thiel has also been a proponent of seasteading, the idea of creating autonomous communities on the ocean, and has been involved in various political causes, including supporting Donald Trump's presidential campaign in 2016.

# Life

Peter Thiel was born on October 11, 1967, in Frankfurt, Germany. His family moved to the United States when he was a child, and he grew up in Foster City, California. Thiel attended Stanford University, where he earned a B.A. in Philosophy in 1989. He went on to earn a J.D. from Stanford Law School in 1992.

After law school, Thiel worked as a law clerk for Judge James Larry Edmondson of the United States Court of Appeals for the Eleventh Circuit. He then worked as an attorney at the law firm Sullivan & Cromwell in New York City.

# Career

In 1998, Thiel co-founded PayPal with Max Levchin and others. PayPal was one of the first companies to offer a secure online payment system, and it quickly became popular with eBay users. PayPal went public in 2002 and was later acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion.

After PayPal, Thiel founded Clarium Capital, a hedge fund, and Palantir Technologies, a data analytics company. Thiel also co-founded Founders Fund, a venture capital firm that has invested in companies such as SpaceX, Airbnb, and Lyft.

Thiel has been an early investor in several successful startups, including Facebook, LinkedIn, and Yelp. He was also a member of the board of directors at Facebook from 2005 to 2019.
